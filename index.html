Rites of the Irreducible

ir·re·duc·i·ble  /ˌirəˈd(y)o͞osəb(ə)l/adjective

not able to be reduced, simplified, or processed into a smaller form; resisting categorization, optimization, or repair.

  1. I.The Index The mind categorizing what resists categorization — structure performing its own failure.
  2. II.The Casting Out Language cast into water, grief enacted rather than processed — the body releasing what it carried.
  3. III.The Seeking Forgiveness Mercy is not optimization. The irreducible interior cannot be debugged.
